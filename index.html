About Us

Our Space

Access to our venue and board game collection is
$5 / person, with a 15% discount for groups of 6+ people.

Come by, find a table, and stay for as long as you’d like. The venue fee also grants you a free hour of PC/console access for the same day (expires at closing).

Just need a space to study or get some work done? No problem! Come take advantage of our wifi, power outlets, and all-day food menu

Digital Entertainment

PC access is $5 / hour — prepaid hours stay on your account so you can come back any time to keep playing. Buy a day pass for only $15.

Our Tier 1 PCs have Ryzen 5 5600X processors, GeForce RTX 4070s, and 144Hz 1440p monitors. We also have a set of Tier 2 PCs (GTX 780, 1080p) at a discounted rate, perfect for web browsing or less demanding games.
